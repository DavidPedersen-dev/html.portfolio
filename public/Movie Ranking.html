David's Top 3 Movies

A brief list of my favorites

The Northman

This film by Robert Eggers perfectly captures the brutality of a viking tale while also preserving magic of old norse ways.

Drive

Ryan Gosling creates a stoic, humble hero who can drive anyone anywhere. This film is a basket packed with meloncholy flavored intensity that contrasts well in the hills of Los Angeles.

The Florida Project

The closest you can get to Kissimmee, Florida without getting your nose wet. Willem Dafoe plays yet another brilliant supporting character.
